Who Are We

Nummi racing formed in 2020 in the height of the pandemic. Driver, Patrick Alex, a racing & Subaru BRZ enthusiast and engineer at General Motors Canada, casually reached out to Vinh Pham as they had crossed paths previously at autocross events and Toyota 86 forums about starting a racing team; and endurance racing team. He suggested using Adam (Mann Motorsports) 2013 Scion FRS as a baseline, Adam had been a foreman at a London, ON Toyota dealership and had known of Vinh from previous years when Vinh worked as a technical rep for Toyota Canada Inc.

Add to the mix Patrick's co worker at GM, Hung Nguyen who had previously competed in Ontario 1500 (an event that Vinh was also crewing at) and the grassroots amateur GM x Toyota collab was born. Harken back to the OG GMxToyota collab: New United Motor Manufacturing (a plant that is now used by Tesla) and that the team was using Mann Motorsports' carâ€¦. NUMMI or new united Mann Motorsports International was born.

We're here to have fun, good vibes and take on the challenges of 8+ hour sports car races with very very limited student & salary-person budgets. We are nummi racing.
